# Information Architecture (IA) — Proposed Sitemap & Navigation

## Navigation model (recommended)

Top navigation:

- Platform
- Offerings
- Solutions
- Case Studies
- Resources
- Company
- Book a demo (primary CTA)

Utility:

- Sign in (if needed)

## Sitemap (v1)

- Home
- Platform
  - Overview
  - Offline-first capture
  - Data integrity & validation
  - Reporting & exports
  - Security
  - Integrations
- Offerings
  - Data collection
  - Monitoring & Evaluation (M&E)
  - Data validation
  - Field reporting
  - Tool-only vs Tool + People
- Solutions (by context)
  - Government & development surveys
  - Consumer & market research
  - Health & humanitarian
  - ESG & impact measurement
- Case Studies
  - Case study template pages
- Resources
  - Capability deck (download)
  - Blog / Insights (if active)
  - FAQs (procurement + operations)
- Company
  - About
  - Careers (optional)
  - Contact

## IA notes (what changes from today)

- “Download” becomes a **resource**, not a primary nav item.
- “Products” becomes either:
  - “Platform” (tooling) + “Offerings” (what buyers purchase), or
  - single “Offerings” if you want to keep nav simpler.
- “Projects” becomes “Case Studies” with a standardized structure and outcomes.
